# Lab Track

## Core lab sequence
1. First reception of a test tone using RTL-SDR and HDSDR.
2. IQ recording analysis in MATLAB / Simulink / Python / C++ / GNU Radio.
3. Study of sampling and aliasing in models and real signals.
4. Basic FFT analysis and parameter estimation.
5. Simple digital filtering and frequency shifting.
6. Fixed-point version of the chain.
7. Signal generation and implementation in FPGA/SoC.
8. Verification of the AD9363 RF chain.
9. Reception and transmission of more complex signals.
10. Design of a supporting hardware circuit in KiCad and on a breadboard.
11. Integrated project.
12. Final presentation.

## Report format
- goal;
- equipment;
- parameters;
- procedure;
- plots and screenshots;
- conclusions.
